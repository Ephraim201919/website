CORE VALUES

To act in utmost honesty and having strong moral principles.
b.) Competence
To serve our customers for thier best interest and success.
c.)Accountability
To be accountable in all aspects be it in groups and in the organization itself. To always communicate and share our ideas with our stakeholders for thier satisfaction.
d.) Commitment
Committed to the highest levle of acheivement obtained through competence and pertinent skills.

WHO CAN JOIN?

  • A registered group of 10 members and above.
  • Members must have attained 18 years of age and of sober mind.
  • Members must be economically active entrepreneurial minded and pertiment skills.
  • Groups ready to abide by the law, rules and regulations of T.E.P.

HOW TO JOIN

  • Complete membership registration form
  • Pay registration fee of ksh.200/-
  • Buy a client record book at a fee of Ksh. 150/-
  • Be in a group that is able to meet at least one's a month

CONTRIBUTION

  • Minimum mandatory contribution by all members of ksh.200 per month. But the more a member saves the better to avoid lumpsum saving when one needs a loan

LENDING

For the group to qualify for the lending it must have been in the programme for at least 3 months. All loans advanced to a member must not exceed 3 times of his/her savings. All amount rendered must be fully guaranteed by all members of the group.

OFFICE DEBTS

Groups shall be given OD not exceeding six times of thier table contribution, this is to minimize the time of refund so that other group on line can benefit too.

Advance

Through the guideline, members are adviced that the amount to be taken as advance(Ngumbato) should not exceed Ksh.3,000 per memner.This is to cater for small emergencies that will occur to a member and of which it will attract an interest of 10% per month which is also distributed when surlus are given to members at the end of the financial year.

Risk Fund

This fund is payment done as insurance to all members so as to safeguard groups funds incase of a member demise (This only covers natural death or noramal accidental death.) This is paid once in a year at cost of Ksh.600/-.

GROUP BY LAWS

  • The group should have a registered name.
  • Every member shall register fully to become a committed group member.
  • A risk fund of Ksh.600 per year shall be paid to every member once a year
  • minimum saving for the members shall be Ksh. 200 every month and a fine of Ksh.50 failure to save.
  • The group shall be meeting once a month
  • Elections shall be held after every 2 years. A by elections can be held if an officer vacates office through resigning.Memebrs can also call for a by election if they have negative issues with the present official
  • Signatories of the group shall include chairperson/secretary and treasurer.
  • Every member of every group has equal rights and power to express him/herself. Through T.E.P office members have the right to discuss a member who fails to abide by the group laws
  • Dismissed member shall clear the debts owed to him/her before leaving the group and shall be given back his/her shares without interest
  • Defaulters shall have thier money withdrawn from thier savings with fines assisted by the T.E.p officer. Role of law will be used to recover the balances owed to the group with help of TEP office and local administration
  • A new member joining the group shall register a fresh and shall not be replaced
  • The group committee has to hold an annual general meeting (AGM) with TEP when they shall discuss the affairs of the group
  • Groups shall be giving 50% part of their interest interms of dividends annually according to one's share.
  • Dividend can be added to one's share or reduce his/her debt
  • For safe keeping of records every member shall have his/her own records but also officials shall keep the group records where every member has the right to access.

NOTE:when office debt is granted to the group the advanced limit will remain of Ksh 3000/- to every member as per constistition.
